7. Affordable and clean energy

What can you do?


  • Reduce the amount you have to travel
  • If traveling choose less energy intensive modes such as train over auto and air travel.
  • Choose and invest in more sustainable energy services.
  • Save electricity by plugging appliances into a power strip and turning them off completely when not in use.
  • Take the bike, walk or take public transport to reduce carbon emissions.

    • Read more about how you can act now here.

    Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy for all.


    Energy is crucial for achieving almost all of the Sustainable Development Goals, from its role in the eradication of poverty through advancements in health, education, water supply and industrialization, to combating climate change.
