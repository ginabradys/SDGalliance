UNs SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS

The 17 sustainable development goals made by the UN are a universal call for help. We might not hold the solution to completely fix all these issues, however we can do our best to contribute to make it a bit better. As they say, small steps can lead to big changes.

1. No poverty

2. Zero hunger

3. Good health and well-being

4. Quality education

5. Gender equalty

6. Clean water and sanitation

7. Affordable and clean energy

8. Decent work and economic growth

9. Industry, innovation and infrastructure

10. Reduced inequalities

11. Sustainable cities and communities

12. Responsible consumption and production

13. Climate action

14. Life below water

15. Life on land

16. Peace, justice and strong institutions

17. Partnerships for the goals
