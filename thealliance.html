THE ALLIANCE

Who are we?

The community of SDG Alliance is people who want to be role models, and engcourage and inspire others to engage. We name all our members for "Spires", which means sprout in Norwegian. Combined with "to aspire" we define people who ambitiously seek to inspire others and grow as individuals. Further we have a set of Ambassadors, each of them have a Goal they are particularily passionnate about and therefore have become a front figure. An Ambassador is a Spire who have grown and are elected based on their engagement, to take a lager role in our community.
